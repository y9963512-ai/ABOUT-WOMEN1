ABOUT WOMEN

Hello, readers or anyone searching for information about women. I am a woman myself, and I created this website for several reasons. Men in my family often diminish women "as a joke," and then try to prove their statements with “facts,” even to very young girls. Growing up in an environment where adult men seriously argue with little girls about heavy topics shaped a lot of my worldview. After yet another conversation like this, I decided to dive deeper into these subjects. For me, it is important to know what people think about this website and the information provided here. There may be mistakes — I try to present reliable, evidence-based knowledge, but I cannot be certain I am always correct. If something here is inaccurate or needs to be expanded, I will appreciate your feedback. This website is not intended to offend or criticize men. It exists to give people around me — and around the world — more knowledge about women. For research, presentations, education, discussion, or simply to have stronger arguments based on science. There is still not an enormous amount of material here, but I hope what you read will deepen your understanding of women.
